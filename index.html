About Josh


I am Josh Jenkins. I was born and raised in St. Louis and went to Francis Howell High School in Weldon Spring. After graduation, I went on to study Chemistry at the University of Missouri-Kansas City. Post-graduation, I have now found myself working in Commercial Real Estate.

Hobbies of mine include running and cycling. I enjoy hiking and fishing and pretty much anything outdoors. I watch sports and read in my down time. I have a mother and father that both live in St. Louis, a twin brother who is also here, and a younger brother that now lives in Connecticut for work. Music is also a big passion of mine.
